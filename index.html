Raj Kumar Manna

I am currently a graduate student working with Prof. P B Sunil Kumar and Prof. Ronojoy Adhikari in the Department of Physics at Indian Institute of Technology Madras. Before joining as a research scholar, I did my master degree (M.Sc) in Physics from Department of Physics, IIT Madras and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Physics from Ramakrishna Mission Residential College, Narendrapur, affiliated to University of Calcutta.


My research interests are in the area of non-equilibrium soft condensed matter and biological physics. I use computer simulations to study the complex structures and dynamics of these systems.
